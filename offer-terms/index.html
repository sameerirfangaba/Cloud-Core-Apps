The Offer

01

This Offer provides subscription benefits at a discounted price, for the period specified in the Offer. The subscription will start upon confirmation of the Offer on your Subscriptions page in the Google Play Store.

Duration

02

After you redeem the Offer, you can confirm the Offer Period end date on your Subscriptions page in the Google Play Store. The Offer is available for a limited time — act before it expires.

Requirements & Eligibility

03

The Offer requires a Google Payments account with a qualifying form of payment at sign-up. This Offer is limited to new users and existing members and may not be combined with other offers or promotions.

Recurring Billing & Cancellation

04

On the date your Offer Period ends, your subscription will continue and your payment method will be automatically charged the standard subscription price — until you cancel.

Important Notice

You can cancel at any time. No refunds are available for partial billing periods. To avoid being charged, cancel before the end of the Offer Period.

Payment & Renewal

05

Payment is charged to your Google Account upon purchase confirmation. The subscription automatically renews at the end of each period unless you turn it off at least 24 hours before the current period ends via Google Play Settings.

Secure Processing — All payments are processed securely via Google Play Billing. We never store your credit card or banking information.

Manage Your Subscription

06

To cancel or manage your subscription and disable auto-renewal, follow these steps:

  • 1 Open the Google Play Store on your Android device.
  • 2 Sign in to the correct Google Account linked to your subscription.
  • 3 Open the Menu and tap "Payments & subscriptions."
  • 4 Tap Subscriptions.
  • 5 Select the subscription you want to cancel.
  • 6 Tap Cancel subscription and follow the prompts.
Please Note

Uninstalling the app does not cancel your subscription. You must cancel through Google Play to stop future charges.
